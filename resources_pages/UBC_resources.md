#### Study spaces on campus

- Within the [Computer Science building (ICICS)](https://maps.ubc.ca/?code=ICCS)
    - ICCS 108 (Bullpen - Graduate Student Lounge)
    - ICCS 359 (MDS Lounge)
    - [Book a meeting room](https://my.cs.ubc.ca/docs/booking-room)
- [List of UBC libraries and hours](https://hours.library.ubc.ca/)
    - If you prefer to book a private room to study, check [bookable study spaces](https://services.library.ubc.ca/facilities/bookable-study-spaces/).
- [Informal learning spaces](https://learningspaces.ubc.ca/find-a-space-informal/)


#### Computing library resources

- The [UBC Library](https://www.library.ubc.ca/) provides access across the university to print and electronic books, journals and more.
- The [ICICS/Computer Science Reading Room](https://www.cs.ubc.ca/our-department/reading-room) is an independent departmental reading room located in ICCS 262. Our area of specialization is information technology and you are welcome to visit during opening hours.

#### Printers

- Computer Science building (ICICS): [Printer Locations](https://my.cs.ubc.ca/docs/printer-locations)
- [Complete list of public printers in the libraries](https://services.library.ubc.ca/computers-technology/copy-print-scan/complete-list-of-public-printers/)
    - Note: payment required

#### Student services

- The [About UBC Student Services page]((https://students.ubc.ca/about-student-services/)) provides a comprehensive directory of resources available to students, including academic support, health and wellbeing, community services, and more.
